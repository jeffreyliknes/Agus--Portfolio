I'm an industrial designer with a background in 3D design and a love for making things, furniture, spaces, communities. I work at McNeel Europe providing technical support for Rhino3D, giving courses, hosting webinars, and representing the software at events across Europe.

I've organized and spoken at Rhino User Meetings in Wroclaw, Budapest, London, and Amsterdam, bringing designers together to learn and connect. Teaching is a big part of what I do: I run courses and workshops on 3D design tools and workflows.

Outside work I design furniture, paint, and look for ways to bring good design into everyday life. I'm drawn to projects where digital tools and physical craft meet.
